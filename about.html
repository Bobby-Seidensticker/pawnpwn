Welcome to the Pawn Pwn about page!

The goal of Pawn Pwn is to get your pawn to your opponent's home row. On your turn you can move your pawn or place a wall. Use walls to make your opponent's path longer, however you may not completely prevent them from reaching a goal square. When you are adjacent to your opponent, you can move to any square they can move to.

To start a new game with a with an friend over the web, make sure you are signed in to Pageforest, and make your first move. Pageforest will automatically save your game and adjust the url in your address bar.
